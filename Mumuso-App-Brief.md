MUMUSO PAID MEMBERSHIP & LOYALTY APP
DEVELOPMENT BRIEF
1. Project Overview
Mumuso intends to develop a Paid Membership & Loyalty App to create a recurring revenue
stream,
increase customer loyalty, and build a structured customer database fully integrated with the POS
system.
2. Objectives
- Generate annual recurring revenue via paid memberships.
- Increase customer retention and lifetime value.
- Capture real transaction-level customer data.
- Enable targeted marketing and sales analytics.
3. Membership Model
- Annual paid membership.
- Members receive 10% discount on eligible purchases.
- Unique member ID with QR code.
- Membership expiry and renewal tracking.
4. Customer App Requirements
- iOS & Android app.
- Registration and login.
- Membership purchase & renewal.
- Digital QR membership card.
- Purchase history.
- Push notifications.
- Membership status and savings summary.
5. POS Integration (Critical)
- QR scanned at cashier.
- POS validates membership.
- Discount auto-applied.
- Transaction synced to loyalty system in real time.
- Two-way API integration required.
6. Admin Dashboard
- Member management.
- Sales reporting.
- Renewal tracking.
- Campaign tools.
- Customer analytics.
7. Data Model
- Customer profile.
- Membership status.
- Transaction history.
- Discount applied.
- Store location.
8. Technical Stack
- Mobile: Flutter / React Native.
- Backend: Cloud (AWS/Firebase).
- Database: SQL/NoSQL.
- APIs for POS integration.
- Payment gateway integration.
9. Security
- Encrypted storage.
- Role-based access.
- Audit logs.
10. KPIs
- Active members.
- Renewal rate.
- Member sales ratio.
- Revenue from memberships.
- Average basket uplift.
This system is a POS-driven customer intelligence platform, not a simple marketing app.
